# Disclaimer

KnowMyCouncil is an evidence-led civic transparency project. We aim for accuracy and reproducibility, but the platform and its data may contain errors.

## Accuracy and sources

- We aim to retain and link to original sources where lawful and practical.
- Users should review primary sources when making decisions or forming conclusions.
- Derived data and summaries should be treated as interpretation or computation over sources, not the source itself.

## Community contributions

Community-submitted information may be reviewed, but should not be treated as automatically authoritative.

Where feasible, the platform should distinguish between:

- raw source material (documents, registers, official datasets)
- normalised records (cleaned and structured)
- derived outputs (aggregations, comparisons, narratives)

## Not legal advice

Nothing in this repository or (future) platform is legal advice.

## Not exhaustive

The platform will not be comprehensive. Councils publish data in inconsistent ways, and some information is missing, delayed, or only obtainable via FOI/EIR.

## Non-partisan

The project is non-partisan. The intention is to support accountability through evidence and transparency, not political campaigning.
