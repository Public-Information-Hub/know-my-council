# Funding and sustainability

KnowMyCouncil is a public-interest project. The intention is to keep it open-source and community-contributed, while being realistic about the costs of maintenance.

This document describes possible funding routes. It does not promise that any of these are already set up.

## Possible funding routes

### Donations

We may accept donations to cover:

- hosting and storage costs
- tooling and security scanning
- contributor support (documentation, triage, moderation)

### Grants

We may apply for grants aligned with:

- civic transparency
- accountability and anti-corruption research
- open data infrastructure
- local journalism support

### Sponsorship

We may accept sponsorship where it does not compromise the project's independence, non-partisan standards, or evidence-led mission.

### Partnerships

We may pursue partnerships with:

- civic tech organisations
- universities and researchers
- journalists and investigative teams

Partnerships should be transparent about:

- data provenance
- funding constraints
- editorial independence

## Principles

- No paywall for core public-interest functionality (subject to future sustainability constraints).
- No "sponsored" editorial outcomes.
- Evidence and sources remain primary; opinions and interpretation must be clearly labelled.
